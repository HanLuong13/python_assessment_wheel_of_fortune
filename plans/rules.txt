rules: 
start with 3 players and 3 rounds
beginning of round, the word is diplayed, randomly picked player goes first

first turn for a player
    spin the wheel to guess a consonant or guess the word in case other players went ahead of you
        if they land on bankruptcy, their turn ends
        if they land on lose a turn, their turn ends
        if they land on $, they guess a consonant
            guessing a consonant
                if consonant is not in word, their turn ends
                if consonant is in word, count how many times <x>, they get <x> times 
                    they amount they landed on into their round money and their turn continues
                    go into second turn
                if not consonant, their turn ends
    guess the word
        if they guessed word right, no one gets money for that round
        round ends
        if they guessed the wrong word, their turn ends

second & repeating turn for a player
    if word is limited to only vowels, only option 1 and 3 will be available 
    they can 1. buy a vowel, 2. spin the wheel to guess a consonant or 3.guess the word
        1. buy a vowel
            if they want to buy a vowel, they must have more than $250 in their bank, 
            otherwise they have to rechoose
            if guess is not in word, already guessed, not a vowel, they lose 250 and their turn ends
            if vowel is in word, they lose 250 and continues turn
                if word is complete, player gets to keep $, round ends
        2. spin the wheel to guess a consonant
            spin the wheel 
                if they land on bankruptcy they lose $ for that turn and their turn ends
                if they land on lose a turn, they get the $ for that turn and their turn ends
                if they land on $, they guess a consonant 
            guessing a consonant
                if consonant is in word, count how many times <x>
                    they get $
                    if word is complete, player gets to keep $, round ends
                if guess is not a consonant or consonant is not in word, they get the $ for that turn and their turn ends
        5. guess the word
                if they guess word right, they get the $
                round ends
                if they guessed the wrong word, their turn ends

when a turn ends, the next chronological player goes
when round 1 ends, round 2 begins, round 2 is exact of round 1
when round 2 ends, bonus round begins, player with most in their bank is the only player

third round
    word is displayed with RSTLNE revealed
    player gets to guess 3 consonants and 1 vowel, no cost
    once they make their guess, those consonants and vowel are revealed
    they have 5 seconds to guess the word
        if timer runs out, they lost
        if guessed wrong, they lost
        if guessed right, they win the money in their bank and a prize
