play round function:
    randomly pick word
    display word
    set round money to 0
    set spin money to 0
    menu: player pick from 4 choices
        1. buy a vowel, 
        2. spin the wheel to guess a consenant, 
        3. guess a consenant or 
        4.guess the word
    validate their input from 1-4
    if 1. buy a vowel
        if they want to buy a vowel
            check bank account
            if more than 250, prompt for guess
                validate guess
                if vowel is not in word
                    they lose 250 and their turn ends
                if vowel is in word
                    they lose 250 and continues turn
                    back to menu
            if they don't have 250 in bank
                rechoose from menu
    if 2. spin the wheel to guess a consenant
        spin the wheel
        if wheel segment is bankruptcy
            they lose $ for that round and their turn ends
        if wheel segment is lose a turn
            they get the $ for that round and their turn ends
        if wheel segment is free spin
            they get an extra spin at the end of their turn
        if wheel segment is a number
            add number to spin money
            they guess a consenant
        prompt player for a consenant
        validate input
        if consenant in already guessed consenant list
            tell them already guessed, prompt again
        if consenant is not in word
            they get the $ for that round and their turn ends
        if consenant in word
            count how many times <x>, they get <x> times the current spin $ and their turn continues
            if word is complete
                round ends and add to win list current play name            
            back to menu
    if 3. guess a consenant
        if there's current spin money   
            prompt player for a consenant
            validate input
            if consenant in already guessed consenant list
                tell them already guessed, prompt again
            if consenant is not in word
                they get the $ for that round and their turn ends
            if consenant in word
                count how many times <x>, they get <x> times the current spin $ and their turn continues
                if word is complete
                    round ends and add to win list current play name
                back to menu
        if there isn't current spin money
            tell them spin first, back to menu
    if 4. guess the word
        if they guess word right
            they get the $ for that round and the round ends
            add to win list current play name
        if they guessed the wrond word 
            they get the $ for that round and their turn ends
